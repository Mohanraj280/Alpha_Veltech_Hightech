Assessment Instructions

Please review the guidelines and rules for the assessment carefully.

  • Ensure you are in a quiet environment free from disturbances to maintain focus.
  • Your camera and microphone must remain active throughout the entire assessment for monitoring purposes.
  • Do not navigate away from the test page once the assessment starts, as it may lead to disqualification.
  • No external materials or devices (e.g., books, notes, calculators, or mobile phones) are allowed.
  • The assessment is time-bound; be mindful of the time and pace yourself accordingly.
  • Make sure your internet connection is stable to avoid disruptions during the assessment.
  • Read all questions carefully and make sure you understand them before answering.
  • If you encounter technical issues, contact the support team immediately via the provided contact details.
  • Avoid any form of cheating or dishonesty; the assessment platform uses automated proctoring and plagiarism detection tools.
  • Submit your answers before the timer runs out; once the time is up, the system will automatically submit your responses.
